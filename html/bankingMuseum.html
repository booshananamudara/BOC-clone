INTRODUCTION
The Bank of Ceylon Money and Banking Museum is a museum developed by Bank of Ceylon. It is situated on the 28th floor of the Bank of Ceylon Head Office Building in the Fort area of Colombo

DESCRIPTION
The Museum and its surroundings consists of the following sections: The lobby area, auditorium, the main display area, the coins and currency section, the Bank of Ceylon Section
